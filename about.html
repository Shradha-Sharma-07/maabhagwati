OUR PROFILE

Maa Bhagwati Degree College, affiliated with Lucknow University, is located in Village Bahipara, Hardoi (UP). Established in 2013, it offers B.A. and B.Sc. programs with specializations in Science (Physics, Chemistry, Zoology, Botany, Maths) and Arts (Hindi, English, Sociology, History, etc.). The college provides well-equipped labs, a library, and sports facilities. Focused on holistic development, it emphasizes academics, moral values, and national pride. Admissions require 10+2 qualifications, with 75% attendance mandatory for exams. Scholarships are available for SC/ST/OBC students. The disciplined environment includes strict library and lab rules. Vision of Maa Bhagwati Degree College

To provide quality higher education and foster holistic development among students, empowering them to become responsible, skilled, and morally upright citizens. The college aims to bridge educational gaps in the region, especially for marginalized and economically disadvantaged groups, while promoting academic excellence, cultural values, and social awareness.

Mission of Maa Bhagwati Degree College

  • Holistic Development: Ensure the all-round growth of students by nurturing their intellectual, physical, moral, and social capabilities.
  • Inclusive Education: Offer equitable opportunities to students from SC/ST, minorities, and economically weaker sections.
  • Academic Excellence: Deliver rigorous and relevant curricula in B.A. and B.Sc. streams affiliated with Lucknow University.
  • Ethical Values: Instill principles of integrity, discipline, and patriotism to cultivate ideal citizens.
  • Skill Enhancement: Equip students with practical knowledge and employable skills using modern facilities.
  • Community Engagement: Bridge educational disparities by collaborating with local communities.
  • Environmental Awareness: Encourage ecological responsibility through mandatory environmental studies.

Founder
Maa Bhagwati Degree College

In Fond Remembrance of Late Shri RamChand Tiwari

(20 December 1927 – 29 March 2010)

With utmost reverence:

A true devotee of social upliftment through the light of knowledge, educationist Shri Rajendra Tripathi once said:

"An educated society is the backbone of a strong nation."

In the same spirit, Swami Vivekananda stated:

"Education is the manifestation of the perfection already in man."

Despite not completing higher education himself, he deeply desired accessible, quality education in this remote region to empower the local youth.

Though he didn’t witness it in his lifetime, the school he started — Maa Bhagwati Primary School — now stands as a symbol of growth, offering quality education to the underprivileged.

His dream for a fully established degree college has been realized in the form of Maa Bhagwati Inter College, fulfilling his noble vision.

His ideals continue to inspire us. We carry forward his mission with honor and dedication.

Message from the Management

In today’s changing world, education must evolve too. With the integration of technology, traditional systems are shifting toward modern, quality-driven approaches. There is a pressing need to uplift rural educational institutions to match urban standards.

To build a strong identity, institutions must meet the expectations of students. Education now spans traditional and modern disciplines, making curriculum diversity essential. Our college is working to affiliate with central and recognized universities, tailoring programs to rural students' needs.

We aim to introduce more professional and career-focused courses to provide local youth with employment-oriented education. Despite resource challenges, we are committed to enhancing program diversity and accessibility.

This college was envisioned with the purpose of empowering this remote region through education. Its success depends not only on students but also on the active involvement of faculty and stakeholders.

Yours sincerely,Om Prakash Tiwari
