Welcome to Maa Bhagwati Degree College

Established in 2013, Maa Bhagwati Degree College is committed to providing quality higher education to students in the rural areas of Hardoi district. Affiliated with Lucknow University, we offer both B.A. and B.Sc. programs with various specializations.

Our college focuses on the holistic development of students through academic excellence, moral values education, and extracurricular activities. We particularly emphasize education for backward classes, scheduled castes, scheduled tribes, minorities, economically weaker sections, and girls.
