About RTI

Public Information Officer (PIO)

The Principal of the college generally serves as the Public Information Officer (PIO), unless otherwise notified.

How to File an RTI

  1. Write your request clearly, addressing it to the PIO of the college.
  2. Mention your name, contact info, and the specific information you need.
  3. Attach ₹10 application fee via IPO, DD, or online (if supported).
  4. Submit it to the college’s address or send it via post.

Sample Information You Can Request

  • Student enrollment in each course
  • Staff qualifications and experience
  • Affiliation and recognition documents
  • Annual budget and expenditure details
  • Admission criteria and procedures
  • Available scholarships and welfare schemes
  • Facilities such as labs, library, sports, etc.
