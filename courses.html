Maa Bhagwati Degree College

Affiliated with Lucknow University, the college offers B.A. and B.Sc. programs with subjects like Hindi, English, Sociology, Political Science, Physics, Chemistry, and Botany.

The college provides essential facilities, including a library, labs, and sports facilities.

Admission requires passing marks in 12th grade, and students must maintain at least 75% attendance to be eligible for exams.
